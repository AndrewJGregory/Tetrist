Welcome to Tetrist!

How to play:


  • Use the ASD keys to move a piece left, down, and right respectively.
  • Use QE to rotate the piece counter-clockwise and clockwise.
  • Press the spacebar to pause or resume the game.
  • Form rows with the pieces to score points!
  • The game is over when the pieces overflow the top of the board.
  • Click anywhere outside of this message to get started!
  • Enjoy :)
